SEDAN

A sedan is a passenger car with four doors and a separate trunk.

SUV

An Suv (sport utility vehicle) is a versatile vehicle designed for both on-road and off-road use.

Hatchback

A hatchback is a compact car with a rear door that opens upward to access the cargo area.

Coupe

A coupe is a two-door car with a fixed roof and a sporty design.

Convertible

A convertible is a car with a retractable roof that can be opened or closed.

Pickup

A pickup truck is a vehicle with an open cargo bed at the back.

About CarSpecsHQ


CarSpecsHQ is a canadian-based automobile forum that provides a one-stop platform for car enthusiasts to access comprehensive information about specific cars, including expert car reviews, detailed car comparisons, and specifications. We also offer a link to dealerships for purchasing cars, and provide special discounts to our members.
